## üí° Cost Optimization Tips

1. **Annual billing saves 20%** on most tools
2. **API pricing** is cheaper at scale than subscriptions
3. **Bundle tools** ‚Äî AdCreative.ai includes some image gen
4. **Pass costs to clients** ‚Äî charge at least 3x your tool costs
5. **Use free tiers first** ‚Äî most tools have trials
